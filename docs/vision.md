# Vision

BDFA exists to make personal finance feel simple, calm, and controlled.

## Product promise

Know where every dollar belongs.

## Principles

BDFA's durable product principle is documented in `docs/product-principles.md`: Financial Truth Over Financial Sales. Every feature should help the user better understand their finances or make a better financial decision.

1. Apple-simple UI.
2. Show purpose, not just balances.
3. Mobile-first from day one.
4. iPhone and iPad must both feel native.
5. Auto-update whenever possible.
6. Manual fallback when banks or aggregators fail.
7. No clutter, no guilt, no spreadsheet energy.

## Signature metric

**Available to Allocate**

This is the money left after bills, reserves, goals, and obligations are already accounted for.
